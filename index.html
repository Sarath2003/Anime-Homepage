Main Character

Uzumaki Naruto

Uzumaki Naruto is a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uzumaki clan. He became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails — a fate that caused him to be shunned by most of Konoha. Naruto worked hard to gain the village's acknowledgement all the while chasing his dream to become Hokage.
